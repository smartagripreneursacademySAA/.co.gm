About Us

Smart Agripreneurs Academy is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting youth agripreneurship in schools and rural communities. Our mission is to inspire and equip young people to realize their full potential in agriculture.

Our Vision

Attracting and retaining youth in agribusiness ventures to make them employable and job creators for sustainable livelihoods.

Our Mission

Encouraging youth to pursue and develop successful careers in the agribusiness industry with an agripreneurial mindset through education and mentoring.

Our Programs

  • Awareness-Raising Seminars on Agribusiness & Climate-Resilient Farming
  • Project-Based Learning for Schools & Rural Communities
  • Agri-Tech Integration (Drones, Smart Irrigation, and Data Monitoring)
  • Entrepreneurship Training for Youth in Agriculture

Our Impact

At Smart Agripreneurs Academy*, we are transforming agriculture through education, mentorship, and technology adoption.

Through our programs:

  • We have trained over 500 students in agripreneurship.
  • Our initiatives have helped rural schools integrate practical agriculture education into their curriculum.
  • We have introduced Agri-Tech solutions like drone farming, automated irrigation, and climate-smart agriculture.

Smart Agripreneurs Academy is committed to educating young people and smallholder farmers to create profitable and sustainable agribusiness ventures Transforming Agriculture in The Gambia: Empowering Youth through Agribusiness Education. Mission Statement We are dedicated to revolutionizing agriculture in The Gambia by equipping young people with the essential agribusiness skills, knowledge, and hands-on experience needed to thrive in the sector. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical education and real-world agricultural practices, fostering a new generation of agripreneurs who will drive innovation, enhance food security, and contribute to sustainable economic growth. Other impact to Date - Vegetable Production: Successfully transplanted over 2 million vegetable seedlings and produced 10,000 kg of vegetables, contributing to local food security. - Youth Engagement: Educated over 2,000 students on sustainable agricultural practices and climate change strategies. - Community Outreach: Provided mentorship, career guidance, and field exposure to inspire youth to pursue careers in agriculture. Approach 1. Project-Based Learning: Students engage in hands-on projects that simulate real-world agricultural challenges, fostering problem-solving and critical thinking skills. 2. Mentorship and Career Guidance: Experienced agripreneurs and educators provide one-on-one mentorship to guide students in their career paths. 3. Field Exposure: Regular visits to farms and agribusinesses offer students practical insights into the agricultural value chain. 4. Climate-Smart Training: Workshops and training sessions focus on sustainable practices, such as water conservation, renewable energy use, and organic farming. Future Goals - Expand the program to reach 5,000 students annually by 2027. - Establish a network of youth-led agribusinesses across The Gambia. - Partner with local and international organizations to scale up climate-smart agricultural practices. - Develop a digital platform to provide online resources and training for aspiring agripreneurs. Call to Action We invite stakeholders, donors, and partners to join us in transforming agriculture in The Gambia.
